namaḥ śākyamunaye buddhāya(1)upāyakauśalyakṛpāluśākya-vaṃśyaṃ hatānyāhatamārasenam|suvarṇamerūpamacārukāya-menaṃ
hi śākyādhipatiṃ namāmi||ādau samutpāditabodhicittoyo jñānapuṇyobhayasaṃbhṛtaḥ
san|udāralīlābhirihādya kālejātaṃ jagannāthamahaṃ stavīmi

(2)devān vināyyātha vinītakālaṃjñātvā'vatīryāpi ca devalokāt|kukṣiṃ viśantaṃ gajavad hi māyā-devyā
vilokyaiva kulaṃ namāmi

(3)janiṃ bhajantaṃ khalu lumbinīvaneśākyātmajaṃ vai daśamāsapūrtau|brahmendravandyaṃ varalakṣaṇaṃ
cabodheḥ kule taṃ niyataṃ namāmi

nṛsiṃhamaṅge magadhe ca śilpaṃpradarśayantaṃ balinaṃ kumāram|nigṛhya cāhaṃkṛtinaḥ samastānatulyakarmāntakaraṃ
namāmi

(4)dharmānuvṛttyai jagato hyavadya-hānyāyupāye kuśalatvapūrvam|antaḥpure yaḥ kṛtasaṃnivāsaḥtaṃ
rājyarakṣāpravaṇaṃ namāmi

niḥsāratāṃ sāṃsṛtikīṃ vilokyatyaktvā gṛhaṃ vyomapathena gatvā|pravrājayantaṃ nijamātmanā taṃviśuddhacaityābhimukhaṃ
namāmi

(5)matvodyamenaiva hi bodhisiddhiṃvarṣāṇi ṣaḍ duṣkaracāriṇaṃ tam|nairañjanānte khalu vīryapāraṃdhyānottamaprāptamahaṃ
namāmi

anādikaṃ sārthayituṃ prayāsam,bodhidrumānmāgadhikād adhastāt|paryaṅkabaddhaṃ hyacalābhibodhimsambodhisamprāptikaraṃ
namāmi

(6)śīghraṃ jagat kāruṇiko'valokyavārāṇasītyādivarasthaleṣu|vineyakān vartitadharmacakraṃpraveśayantaṃ
trinaye namāmi

ṣaṭ tīrthyaśāstṝnatha nigrihītuṃyo devadattādyaparān kudṛṣṭīn|vārāṇasīye'damayad hi māraṃ,muniṃ
jayantaṃ samare namāmi

(7)guṇaiḥ pradarśya tribhave'tulārhaṃśrāvastike prātihāryam mahad yaḥ|supūjito devamanuṣyayonibhiḥ|taṃ
śāsanasyarddhikaraṃ namāmi

kusīdinaḥ prerayituṃ suśīghraṃ,pravitrakauśīnagarīyabhūmau|tyaktvāmaraṃ vajrasamānakāyaṃnamāmi
yātaṃ parinirvṛtiṃ tam

(8)samyaktayā santamapi hyamartyaṃ,puṇyaṃ labheranniti bhāvisattvāḥ|vikṛtya tatkālamanekadhātūnaṣṭāṃśadhātūn
dadataṃ namāmi

evaṃ prakārairbhagavat praśāstṛ-līlāsamāsastutijātapuṇyaiḥ|(9)samastasattvācaraṇaṃ hyapīdaṃyathaiva
bhūyāt sugatasya caryā
